## "This is Not an Official Site": Theological Speculation and Discussion on the LDS Freedom Forum 20 October, 2023 Levi Sands, University of Iowa (levi-sands@uiowa.edu) Amy Chapman, Arizona State University Spencer P. Greenhalgh, University of Kentucky
## Terminology Disclaimer Despite our data mostly consisting of members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints we will use the terms Mormon/Mormonism as they are common in the academic literature and as a way to acknowledge the broader Latter-day Saint movement
## Mormon Theology - Latter-day Saints don't have a systematic theology (Blythe 2020) - Mythology and narrative theology inherited from Joseph Smith (Flake 2007) - Personal revelation and doctrine as taught by church leaders can come in conflict (Knoll and Riess 2020) - These characteristics of Mormon theology leaves many areas of differing beliefs, speculations and areas for discussion
## Data - Collected all posts made to the LDS Freedom Forum - From the inception in 2005 through December 2021 - Over 800,000 posts
## Methods - Topic Modeling - Topic Modeling As a Method of Analysis - Several steps to get topic model outputs, each of those steps have tradeoffs: embedding, dimensionality reduction, clustering, tokenization, weighting scheme, and sometimes more (Grootendorst 2022) - Newer pre-trained embedding models use context to keep more semantic information (Devlin et al. 2019) - "'[T]opics' (clusters of words associated with a single theme) emerge inductively based on patterns of co-occurrence of words in a corpus." (Supran and Oreskes 2021 p 713) ![https://developers.google.com/static/machine-learning/guides/text-classification/images/WordEmbeddings.png](../../pics/OneOffPresentations/2023_10_23_Levi_Sands_SSSR_This_Is_Not_An_Official_Site/WordEmbeddings.webp)

Methods - Topic Modeling

  • Used topic modeling to sort posts into hierarchical topics
    • Resulted in 646 topics

## Methods - Qualitatively Selecting Theological Topics - Qualitative selection of topics - Based on the top key words/phrases that touched on theological topics - Preference for topics not explicity political - Resulted in 103 topics that focus on theological topics - Identified 5 candidate topics - Confirmed theological nature of posts for small number of select topics - Selected 3 threads for each topic from list of threads with the highest number of posts assigned the target topic
## Methods - Qualitative Coding - The 3 threads for the first topic (polygamy) produced code saturation - Reviewed 3 threads (602 posts) to become familiar with data - Developed proto-codes based on notes from review - "Theological Ontology" - "Theological Epistemology" - Next steps: - Develop formal codebook - Formal analysis of posts
